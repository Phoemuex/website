Research interests

I am interested in Functional analysis and Harmonic analysis, in particular in
  • Function spaces and their embeddings
  • Multiscale systems (wavelets, shearlets and generalizations)
  • Coorbit theory
  • Time-frequency analysis
  • Fourier analysis
As a mathematical hobby, I am interested in almost everything related to measure theory and in trying to crack toy problems like this one.

Biography
